Rules/Policies

Entry Requirements

  • No Skateboards, scooters, bikes, rollerblades, strollers, cleats, pets, food, etc. allowerd in the Warrior Recreation Center (WRC)

  • Present front desk with:

    • Proper University of Hawaii identification with validation, membership card, or guest pass

      • Transcripts, proof of tuition, MyUH, Laulima, etc. are not accepted.

  • Proper athletic attire

    • Workout towel at least 12" by 12"

    • Buttons, zippers, belts, buckles and rivets of any kind are prohibited

    • Attire must be clean and in good condition

    • Shoes must completely enclose the entire foot

      • Nonmarking shoes only

    • Tank tops, T-shirts, longsleeve shirts, and jackets are allowed provided that they adequately cover body and meet dress code requirements

    • Muscle tank tops, midriff are prohibited

    • Spandex, shorts and pants are allowered provided that they adequately cover body and meet dress code requirements

  • Nonglass, sealable containers are permitted in the WRC

Buddy Pass Policy

  • Those with access to the Warrior Recreation Center (WRC) can sponsor a guest using the buddy pass:

    • Daily or weekly buddy passes are available for purchase.

    • Sponsors purchasing a buddy pass are limited to ONE guest per buddy pass.

    • Guests will NOT be allowed to change their original sponsor written on their assigned WRC paper pass.

    • The sponsor must be present with the buddy at all times when entering and using the facility. The sponsor may not leave the facility without the buddy!

    • Guests must present a valid photo ID (driver’s license, state ID, passport, etc.) during purchase and when
      entering the facility. THIS DOES NOT INCLUDE HIGH SCHOOL ID’s!

    • Guests must be at least 16 years old to use the facility, and anyone under the age of 18 years old are required to attain a signature from a parent/guardian on their membership form.
